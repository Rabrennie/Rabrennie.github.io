Projects

Play Genius

Play Genius is a chrome extension that adds a lyrics button next to the queue button in Google Play Music.

Lyrics from Genius.com open in google play music and allow you to click on annotated lyrics to view their meaning.

Simplicity

Simplicity is a minimalistic 3d puzzle game.

You play as a cube who has to get to the goal tile in the least number of steps possible. There are multiple types of obstacles.
